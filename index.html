about us

Sault-Health.com is a not-for-profit organization aimed at raising awareness and holding accountable our healt-care system and health care system providers as well as those in charge of overseeing and maintaning public and private health-care.

While the world transitions to a more secure and transparent society we have to accept that some evils were once necessary where we once lacked the ability to provide true accountability and transpanrency there were also nefarious actors. Today thanks to encryption and other technologies these for profit bad actors are having their hands cleaned, and it is thanks to people like YOU!

Speak up and be heard

Sault-Health is a bi-product of an across-the-board failing health care system in Canada, and aims at becoming the online resource for those who have been neglected and or abused by local health-care providers and have fallen through the cracks in a system that allows for lack of oversight and true accountability. Sault-Health has conducted several independent surveys and is actively collecting data from the public. Complaints shared with Sault-Health.com will be kept anonymous please stay tuned as we work out the details for our anonymous community board.

Wether your complaint is regarding a private or public health care operator, from front-line overworked staff to the head CEO your complaint matters! Its time for some accountability and oversight.
